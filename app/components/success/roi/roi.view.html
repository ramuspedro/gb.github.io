Programme introduction

This program emphasizes the ROI Methodology developed by industry leader Dr. Jack Phillips. Participants will experience the application of the ROI Process model. This includes developing application impact objectives, isolating the effects of the program, converting data to monetary values, tabulating the appropriate program costs, and calculating the ROI.
