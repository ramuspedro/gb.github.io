Workshop introduction

Digital Badges are quickly becoming the standard for tracking and validating skills and achievements in the corporate world. Badges are emerging as an accepted representation of professional credentials over traditional paper certificates.

A Digital Badge is a visual representation of the achievements, skills, competencies you will earn before, during and after the ROI workshop. Represented by a graphical image, Digital Badges contain comprehensive data to authenticate the badge ownersâ€™ credentials, by including information on who earned the badge, its criteria, learning evidence, and who endorsed it.

At Greenbooks, we translate your learning outcomes, achievements and contributions into web-enabled, shareable digital badges.

Challenge

To make the ROI process of learning fun and competitive.

Solution

Greenbooks came up with an idea for Digital Badges. These badges would act as rewards for the participants for the completion of various tasks and stages of learning. These badges can be displayed by the participants on their social media accounts as accomplishments.

Results

Increase in the number of participants applying the ROI methodology at work.
Increase in the participants taking the tasks seriously and on a timely manner.
