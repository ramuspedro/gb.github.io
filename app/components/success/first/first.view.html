challenge

India’s Largest domestic courier company: 930 offices in India; 10,000 plus employees; 220 countries served globally.

First Flight’s sales target for this financial year is 100 crores, against an achieved target of 50 crores in the last financial year. With an increased revenue target, First Flight also faces challenges in recruiting the right people, motivating its staff to work in the outskirts of the city, where the business is booming, and expanding customer relationships.

solution

  2. Set clear training goals and success objectives
  3. Support for First Flight in recruiting the right team

Product and process training

  1. Induction training on Process & Products
  2. Fundamental selling skills training

Coaching

  1. Continuous on-the-job coaching
  2. Track improvement process

Evaluation and ROI

  1. Evaluate training feedback
  2. Measure Return On Investment for the training
