Conducting productive meetings

Organize and conduct meetings that are effective and interactive to gain time and increased productivity. By the end of this program, you will master the techniques and skills to prepare, communicate, conduct and conclude meetings. With a strong focus on communication, clarity, dealing with difficult situations and exploring the best of the talent around, this program has the potential to change meetings in your organization.

Is it right for me?

Suitable for all individuals who are required to run, conduct and lead any kind of meeting successfully.
