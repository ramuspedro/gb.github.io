Business writing

People represent the face of any organization. Any written document that is sent has to establish credibility and professionalism. Appropriate for professionals to write competently at any level, this course helps to plan the writing, perfect the grammar, and assess the style and tone of an email. Combining gamification, e-learning and personalized coaching, the learner is invited to an engaging learning experience.

Is it right for me?

Appropriate for professionals at any level, whose role requires a lot of email and report writing.
