Approach

Our front-loaded custom training process begins with a clear focus on the outcome. Our approach to business alignment ensures your investment drives business results.

1. Identify stakeholder needs ROI Objectives

We begin by identifying specific business measures that, if improved, will take advantage of the opportunity or the problem. By working through the process, we map the performance behaviors or actions to improve the business measures. Then we will identify the learning needs and the preferred methodologies for learning.

2. Develop Objectives

We build objectives & success measures to the stakeholder needs at different levels (business impact objectives, application objectives, learning objectives and reaction objectives). Objectives set the stage for program evaluation, ensuring the right measures are taken based on the results that are important to stakeholders.

Our training evaluation model captures and reports the chain of impact that occurs from the program.
