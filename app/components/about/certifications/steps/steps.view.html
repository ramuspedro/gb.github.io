Steps to earn your badges

1Register for workshop

The first stage of your ROI journey involves registering for the workshop and unlocking the pre course material by earning the ROI kick-starter badge. This material will equip you for the upcoming workshop.

2Attend ROI workshop

Once you have registered for the workshop and have prepared for the workshop with the pre course material, you will need to attend the two day workshop on the ROI methodology. Badges will be awarded according to the conditions satisfied (Punctuality, Consistency, Enthusiasm, etc.).

3Post training support

Post training, you will be collaborating with your fellow ROI participants in implementing the ROI methodology. This form of collaborative learning will earn you the ROI Collaborator badge.

4Earn the mastery

Finally, once you have fully absorbed the ROI principles and have efficiently applied them at your workplace by executing the project plan that you had drafted and presented at the workshop, you will be awarded the coveted ROI Mastery badge.
