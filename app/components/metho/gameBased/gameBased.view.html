Game-based

60% of learners are motivated by progress mechanics such as leader boards or badges and friendly competition.

Gamification, or game-based learning, is about making use of game mechanisms to break big learning requirements into achievable, scaffolded challenges, so that the making of progress becomes much easier and enjoyable, while providing the opportunity for friendly competition, instant feedback and collaborative problem-solving.

This type of learning is designed to reach more learners in more places via laptops, tablets and smartphones, with the strategic use of theatrical concepts and animation.

Besides using this methodology on our own customized programs, we can apply it to any existing program that your organization has to offer.
