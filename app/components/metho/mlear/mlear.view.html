M-learning

80% of people access internet through their cellphones and 70% of the companies worldwide plan on adopting M-learning in the next 2 years.

Mobile learning involves the use of mobile technology to enable learning anytime and anywhere at a high engagement rate. Learning can unfold in a variety of ways: people can use mobile devices to access educational resources, connect with others, or even create content.

Mobile learning also encompasses efforts to support broad educational goals and improve communication with learners. Besides all the benefits of on-demand learning, another great application is mobile simulation based training.
